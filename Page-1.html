HOW MY INTEREST FOR CODING BEGAN

My love for technology began back in high school when I met an alumna who came back to visit my school on the annual Seniors’ Day. She came to speak to us as a career guidance counselor; to talk about her success story after graduating from school. Up until that point, I had no idea what I wanted to be, and her story inspired me. At the time, she told us that she was working as a Senior Software Engineer for a reputed multinational organization and that now, she retired her parents and provided for them, and so my dream began... 

COMMERCE VS COMPUTER SCIENCE

I chose to pursue my Advance Levels in the Commerce Stream despite wanting to become a software engineer because I knew the knowledge I'd accumulated would come in handy when understanding and catering to clients as a software engineer in the future. Commerce and Technology go hand in hand so I knew my knowledge on the business aspect of things could one day help me disersify my skill set and branch out in the IT field...
